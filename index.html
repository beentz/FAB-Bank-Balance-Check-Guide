What Is an FAB Balance Check?

FAB Bank balance check is a banking service provided by First Abu Dhabi Bank that allows customers to view their current account or card balance at any time. It helps users monitor available funds, confirm recent transactions, and keep track of money coming in and going out of their account in real time.

This service is especially important for salary account holders, Ratibi card users, and prepaid card customers who want to quickly verify salary deposits or card balances without visiting a branch. Whether accessed online or through offline channels, an FAB balance check gives an accurate snapshot of your financial position, making day-to-day money management simpler and more controlled.

FAB’s secure online systems make balance checks both convenient and safe, ensuring your financial data remains protected while being accessible whenever you need it. This combination of speed, control, and security makes FAB balance checks an important part of smart banking in 2026.

Top Methods to Check FAB Balance (2026)

FAB provides multiple online and offline options to check your balance, catering to regular bank accounts, salary cards, Ratibi cards, prepaid cards, and Magnati-managed cards. You can choose a method based on convenience, internet availability, and the type of FAB card or account you use.

1. FAB Balance Check Through Website (Online Banking)

FAB online banking allows you to check your account balance securely through the official website.

  1. Visit the official FAB online banking portal
  2. Log in using your User ID and password
  3. Select your account or card from the dashboard
  4. View your available balance and recent transactions

This method is ideal for users who want detailed account information on a larger screen.

2. FAB Balance Check Using FAB Mobile App

The FAB mobile app is one of the fastest ways to check balances for savings accounts, salary accounts, and prepaid cards.

  1. Download and open the FAB Mobile App
  2. Log in using your registered credentials or biometric access
  3. Tap on the account or card section
  4. Check your real-time balance and transaction summary

3. FAB Balance Check Through ATM (Offline Method)

  1. Insert your FAB debit or prepaid card into the ATM
  2. Enter your PIN
  3. Select “Balance Inquiry”
  4. View your balance on screen or print a receipt

4. FAB Balance Check Using PPC Magnati Portal

  1. Visit the official Magnati balance inquiry portal
  2. Enter your card details as required
  3. Complete the verification process
  4. View your available balance

5. FAB Balance Check Using Customer Care

  1. Call the FAB customer care helpline
  2. Choose the balance inquiry option
  3. Verify your identity as requested
  4. Receive your balance details from the agent or automated system

6. FAB Balance Inquiry via SMS

  1. Send the prescribed balance inquiry SMS format
  2. Use the mobile number registered with FAB
  3. Receive your balance details via reply message

FAQs: Related to FAB Balance Enquiry

1. How can I check my FAB balance?
You can check your FAB balance through online banking, the FAB mobile app, ATMs, Magnati portals, SMS services, or customer care.

2. What is the minimum balance in FAB?
The minimum balance depends on your account type. Salary and savings accounts have different requirements set by FAB.

3. How do I check my FAB salary card balance?
You can check it via the FAB mobile app, Magnati portal, ATM, or customer care.

4. Is there a fee for balance inquiry?
Most online and app-based balance checks are free. ATM or SMS charges may apply in some cases.

5. What is the SWIFT code of FAB?
The SWIFT code of First Abu Dhabi Bank is NBADAEAA.

6. How to check Ratibi Salary Card Balance?
Ratibi card balances can be checked using the FAB mobile app, Magnati portal, ATM, or SMS service if activated.

7. How to activate my FAB debit card?
You can activate it via the FAB mobile app, ATM, or by contacting customer care.

8. How to activate a FAB prepaid card?
Activation can be done through the FAB app, Magnati portal, or customer support, depending on the card type.

9. How to register in FAB Mobile Banking?
Download the FAB app, select registration, verify your details with OTP, and create login credentials.

10. How to open a FAB iSave account?
You can open an iSave account through the FAB mobile app or online banking by completing the digital application.

11. Does FAB have a zero balance account?
Yes, FAB offers zero-balance options mainly for salary accounts, subject to eligibility.

12. How to check transaction history in the FAB app?
Log in to the FAB app, select your account or card, and view the transaction history section.
